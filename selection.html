Skikda (Btifinagh: ⵙⴽⵉⴽⴷⴰ) (in the Amazigh: Tseda-1) called ancient Rusikada according to the Phoenician name, the state is located east of the Algerian coastal strip at a length of approximately 140 km, and it is the capital of Skikda state characterized by splendid beaches in camels, and Skikda port is currently one of the main ports in Algeria .
